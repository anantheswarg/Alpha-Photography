About Me!

Hello! This is Ananth. I'm a Software Architect, passionate about photography. I have been making photos since many years now. I like capturing everything around, especially people at their best, kids, landscapes, candid moments and fashion. I like to look at things in a more dramatic way as reality is not good enough. This is a life time pursuit for me and I'm happy to have recieved multiple awards/ publications for photography in the process. I hope to keep doing this as long as I can.
